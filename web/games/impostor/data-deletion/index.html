Account and Data Deletion Request - Impostor

In compliance with Google Play data safety policies and the GDPR, users of the Impostor app (developed by W2C / Where2Create) have the right to request the total deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request deletion

To delete your account and all associated information, simply send an email to:

contact@where2create.com

  • Subject: Delete Impostor Account
  • Content: Please indicate the email address you used to sign in to the app (Google Sign-In).

What data is deleted?

Once your request is processed (within a maximum period of 7 business days):

  • Your library user record will be deleted from our Firebase Auth servers.
  • Any profile information or settings saved in the cloud connected to your user ID will be deleted.

Important: By deleting your account, you will permanently lose access to any premium features linked to that specific account, with no possibility of recovery.

Data we retain

We do not retain personal data after deletion. Only financial transaction records (purchases) are kept anonymously for accounting and legal purposes in accordance with tax regulations.
