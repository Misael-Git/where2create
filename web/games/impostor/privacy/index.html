1. Information Collection and Use

To provide a better experience when using our Service, we may collect certain technical information. Below we detail what data we collect and for what purpose.

A. Personally Identifiable Information
The App does not proactively request or collect personally identifiable information from the user, such as real name, physical address, email, or phone number.

B. Technical and Usage Data (Diagnostics and Analysis)
The App may automatically collect certain technical information for performance metrics and error detection. This information includes:

  • Device Internet Protocol (IP) address.
  • Operating System type and version.
  • General information about the device hardware.
  • Crash logs (Crashlytics) and usage statistics within the app.
  • Device advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID or IDFA). Please note that these identifiers may be considered personal data under certain regulations, such as GDPR. Their use is limited exclusively to displaying advertising and analyzing ad performance.

2. Legal Basis for Processing

For users located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the processing of your data is based on the following legal grounds under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR — Regulation EU 2016/679):

  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6.1.f GDPR): For the collection of technical diagnostic data (crash logs, usage statistics) in order to maintain and improve the performance of the App.
  • Consent (Art. 6.1.a GDPR): For the use of device advertising identifiers for personalized advertising purposes. Adult users may manage or withdraw their consent at any time from their device settings or, where appropriate, through the consent panel integrated into the App.

3. Children's Privacy (Under 13 years old)

Our App is designed to be a safe environment suitable for all audiences, including children under 13 years old. We are highly committed to compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Google Play Families Policy Requirements.

  • Zero personal data collection: We do not request or store personally identifiable information from children under 13 years old.
  • Safe advertising for children: If we show advertisements to users identified as children, or if the app is directed at them, we exclusively use Family Certified Ad Networks approved by the Google Play Store. These networks ensure that ads are age-appropriate and disable interest-based advertising (behavioral tracking).
  • No advertising identifiers for minors: Under no circumstances will device advertising identifiers be used when the user is under 13 years old or when the App operates in child-friendly mode.

If you are a parent or legal guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information accidentally, please contact us at the email listed at the end of this document, and we will take the necessary steps to remove it immediately from our servers.

4. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding the processing of your personal data:

  • Right of access: You may request a copy of the data we process about you.
  • Right to rectification: You may request the correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): You may request the deletion of your data when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.
  • Right to restriction of processing: You may request that we limit the use of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: You may request that we provide your data in a structured, commonly used format.
  • Right to object: You may object to the processing of your data based on legitimate interest.
  • Right to withdraw consent: When processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Right to lodge a complaint: If you believe that the processing of your data violates regulations, you may file a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD) or with the supervisory authority of your country of residence.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through the email indicated in Section 11 of this document.

We retain collected technical data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy:

Once these periods have expired, the data is deleted or irreversibly anonymized.
