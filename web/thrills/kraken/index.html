Engineering Study 01

Kraken.

Battle with the Abyss.

The Kraken is a high-speed hypercoaster designed to simulate a physical battle with the legendary sea monster.

Intense G-forces and record-breaking drops make this our most extreme experience yet. This concept explores the psychological impact of scale and depth in physical attractions.

Max Speed 120 KM/H
Record Height 75 M
Intensity 4.5 G
Concept State Active
